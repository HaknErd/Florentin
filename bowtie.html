The Bowtie Structure

The Web and how it is connected is often represented as a Bowtie diagram


The web is made up of three main parts (visible in the diagram):

- The Strongly Connected Core (SCC) which are websites which link to others but are also linked to

- The IN pages which are websites which link to others (new pages)

- The OUT pages which are websites that are only linked to (reference pages)


There are also minor parts like Tendrils which

connect to the IN and OUT pages but not to the SCC
